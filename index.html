About Us

Welcome to Shree Banashankari Public School,

A proud initiative of the Shree Banashankari Lingayat Education and Rural Development Society (R) Savalagi, established in 2013. Our journey began in 2010 with a vision to provide quality education and foster holistic development in the rural community.

Since our inception, Banashankari Public School has grown steadily, and we are delighted to have witnessed remarkable progress. From a modest start with 10 students, we have now flourished into a vibrant educational institution with a thriving student body of 300. This growth is a testament to our commitment to nurturing young minds and providing them with a conducive learning environment.

At Banashankari Public School, we prioritize academic excellence, character development, and overall well-being.Our dedicated faculty and staff work tirelessly to create an enriching educational experience for every student. We believe in instilling values that go beyond the classroom, preparing our students for success in both academics and life.

In addition to academic excellence, we also offer a range of co-curricular activities and sports programs, providing students with opportunities to explore their interests, develop new skills, and build friendships. We also have a vibrant parent-teacher association, which works closely with school administration to support student learning and development.

Join us on this educational journey as we continue to inspire, educate, and empower the future leaders of tomorrow. Explore our website to learn more about our programs, facilities, and the unique opportunities we offer at Banashankari Public School.

Mission

"To provide equitable and accessible education of the highest quality to all students, regardless of their background, and to prepare them for academic success, citizenship, and lifelong learning."

Vision

"To be a leader in innovative and inclusive education, where all students are empowered to reach their full potential and make positive contributions to their communities and the world."

Objective

"To showcase the school's culture and values, such as its commitment to student-centered learning, inclusivity, diversity, and community involvement and to enhance communication and collaboration."
